Dev Weekends Club at NUML

Introduction

The Dev Weekends Club at NUML is a vibrant student-led community dedicated to fostering technical skills, collaboration, and innovation among students. Our club provides a platform for learning, networking, and personal growth in the world of software development and technology.

Mission

Our mission is to empower NUML students to become confident developers, creative problem-solvers, and future tech leaders by providing hands-on experiences, mentorship, and opportunities to work on real-world projects.

Activities

  • Weekly coding sessions and workshops
  • Guest lectures from industry professionals
  • Hackathons and coding competitions
  • Project showcases and demo days
  • Mentorship programs for beginners
  • Open-source contribution drives
  • Networking events and meetups

Membership

Membership is open to all NUML students passionate about technology, coding, and innovation. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, you are welcome to join and contribute.

Benefits

  • Access to exclusive workshops and resources
  • Opportunities to work on real-world projects
  • Mentorship from experienced developers
  • Networking with peers and industry experts
  • Recognition for contributions and achievements
  • Personal and professional growth

How to Join

  1. Fill out the online registration form (link provided by club coordinators).
  2. Attend an orientation session to learn about club activities.
  3. Participate in club events and start your journey!
